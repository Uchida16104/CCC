### Dear Human, thank you for your interest in this call.  
The Cookie Collective is calling for contributions for a collective visual release on the theme DIVAGATION:

\- The act of wandering here and there, out of place.  
\- The action of a mind wandering, wandering at the whim of its reverie.  
\- The contravention of letting animals wander or abandoning them on the roads.  
\- Displacement, total or partial, permanent or temporary, of a river bed.  
Synonyms: elucubration - extravagance - incoherence - rambling - reverie - daydream.

In this compilation, we welcome propositions conceived using alternative DIY techniques: shaders, livecoding, analog video, photogrammetry, stop motion, moding, collages, generative, rotoscoping, compositing, infrared, underwater, foundfootage,Super-8 ...

You can apply with works already created.

#### Dear human, thx for the interest you give to this call for proposals.

We start the second phase of the **Cookie Collective Compilation.**

For the first phase, we asked participants to send us visual propositions, produced through non conventional techniques.

For this second phase we want to ask you to compose the soundtrack for the videos of this first phase.

You can see all the details of this first phase here

#### The theme of this compilation is the DIVAGATION concept :

      • The act of wandering here and there, out of place.

      • The action of a mind wandering, wandering at the whim of its reverie.

      • The contravention of letting animals wander or abandoning them on the roads.

      • Displacement, total or partial, permanent or temporary, of a river bed.

      • Synonyms: elucubration - extravagance - incoherence - rambling - reverie - daydream.

Once you’ve filled in this form, confirming your wish to take part, we will send you two folders containing each: a video, its title, the technique used and some information about it’s creator.

Your mission will then be to compose some soundtrack for the video you have chosen that either directly matches and fit the visual or on the contrary stands in contrast with it, while trying to inhabit the divagation theme.

Don’t hesitate to propose something **crazy** : there is already too much unsurprising content on this planet.

We encourage you to:

      • use **alternative and innovative** production techniques : sound effects, vocal textures, live coding, body percussions, acoustic instruments, or every kind of sound synthesis (granular, additive, concatenative, FM, etc), filed recorded materials, etc.

      • compose in a non-mainstream music style (nothing mandatory, we also like it mainstream) : acousmatic, noise, dungeon synth, breakcore, ambient, hypertone, blackmidi, slam etc.
